Is my case data kept safe and confidential?

Yes, security is our core priority. All uploaded case documents, facts, and drafts are fully encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We enforce strict data isolation; your client data is never used to train or fine-tune public models.

Does JudiQ AI replace professional legal counsel?

Absolutely not. JudiQ is designed as an advanced decision-support tool to assist licensed advocates and in-house legal departments. All AI-generated analyses, strategies, and draft notices must be reviewed and verified by a qualified attorney.

JudiQ User Manual & Strategy Guide

Comprehensive Platform Guide for Litigators

Getting Started with JudiQ

JudiQ is an adversarial legal intelligence platform designed to stress-test case files, find procedural loopholes, and map court survivability.

Litigation Workflow Overview

Traditional legal prep revolves around finding matching precedents. JudiQ reverses this process by actively attempting to dismantle your case using standard opposing counsel defense strategies, allowing you to patch weaknesses before the courtroom trial.

Navigating Your Dashboard

  • Start New Case: Launches the interactive case wizard to input facts, timelines, and evidence files.
  • Quick Mode: Minimal form entry to get immediate probability projections.
  • Smart Upload: Secure batch uploader for OCR document ingestion.
  • Notice/Reply Generator: Opens templates for demand notices, complaints, and responses.

Case Analysis Wizard

Our step-by-step wizard is explicitly optimized for Section 138 Cheque Bounce matters.

1. Step 1: Core Litigation Facts

Select case classifications, fill in filing dates, court names, and basic details. Correct date indexing is crucial for calculating statute limitation periods.

2. Step 2: Smart Document Upload

Upload document scans. The built-in OCR scans for matching cheque amounts, bank dishonour reasons (e.g. signature mismatch, insufficient funds), and demand notice postal delivery receipts.

3. Step 3: Evidentiary Verification

Specify transaction histories and confirm that the statutory 15-day notice period was given before proceeding with case filing.

Legal Drafting & Templates

Automatically generate compliant notice drafts and response letters based on case analysis results.

Drafting Procedure

Upon completing a case analysis, navigate to the **Drafts** tab on your results page. Select the template type (e.g. Legal Notice Sec 138, Complaint Petition, or Reply Brief). The generator will automatically populate client names, dates, amounts, and citations.

Custom Tone Adjustments

Toggle between different argument tones (e.g., Assertive, Professional, or Conciliatory) in the preview side-panel to match your firm's presentation style. You can copy the generated text or print directly to your firm's letterhead.

Confidentiality & Data Security

Legal data demands bank-grade protection. We isolate user workspaces and keep confidential case files secure.

Data Protection Standards

  • Full Encryption: All uploaded files, memos, and case logs are encrypted using symmetric AES-256 before saving to disk.
  • Zero Model Training: We enforce strict tenancy isolation. Your case data is never used to train global AI models.
  • Complete Deletion: Clicking "Delete Case" permanently and irreversibly wipes the files and generated data from all servers.
